COMPANY PROFILE

Guangdong Flexwarm Advanced Materials & Technology Co.,Ltd.

Located at the Creative Industry Park of Zinitang, No.7, Xi’an Road, Shawan Town, Panyu District, Flexwarm is adjacent to Shunde where the appliance industry is well developed. Within ten minutes driving from either Dongxin Highway’s exit or Shunde light rail station, it is easy to access.

Since its establishment, Flexwarm has been focusing on the research and development, as well as production and applications of the thick film technology. It not only provides innovative heating products and sensors with high precision and sensitivity, but also overall solution of high precision control in various harsh environments such as high temperature and high corrosion.

Being an integrated operation service enterprise in the field of clean energy, the company, in research and development, production, sales and services, is committed to providing customers with comprehensive product solutions. It holds the concept of doing everything by heart to provide the best service to each customer.

COMPANY ENVIRONMENT

FACTORY EQUIPMENT

8K Flexwarm

8K Flexwarm was born from a need to bring people closer to the outdoors and help increase participation in outdoor activity.

62% of people state that cold weather is the main barrier stopping them from exploring the great outdoors.

The innovative Flexwarm® heating technology eradicates those barriers by keeping you warm and comfortable no matter what the temperature.

8K Flexwarm is a registered trademark of Flexwarm Europe – led by CEO Scott Brenchley who has over 20 years’ experience in the technology sector and also founded the well-respected and highly successful Tactus Ltd & 8K Xtreme Ltd brands.

Scott is responsible for driving the Flexwarm Europe ethos which focuses creating exceptional, innovative wearables and charging products that are changing the face of the integrated wireless technology industry. We believe that the future is wireless and the possibilities are limitless.
